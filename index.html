This website holds a collection of animations visualising the rotational velocity of a car or multiple cars relative to an observer. This is therefore a means of visualising how an observer would judge the speed of the car. Adding obstructions (in the form of another car) could therefore interfere with the observer's ability to judge this, particularly if the car is accelerating.
