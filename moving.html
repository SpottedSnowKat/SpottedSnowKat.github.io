Obstruction in Motion

In this animation, another moving car obstructs the view of the observer. This moving car has an acceleration of 5m/s2, and is 7m from the observer. While the smaller car behind is fully obscured, the rotational velocity is plotted in red; at other times, it is plotted in green.

 
 
    • These animations are still being refined for access over the internet. They are most reliable in Safari, lag in Edge and Opera and are poor in Chrome, while other browsers are untested.
