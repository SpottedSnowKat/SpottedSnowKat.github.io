Standard Case

This shows a car with constant linear acceleration. The rotational velocity of the car relative to an observer is shown, rather than the linear velocity. Below the car, both the rotational velocity and the linear velocity are shown, while the rotational velocity is also plotted on the graph. For this animation, the observer is 10m away from the path of the car, while the car has a constant linear acceleration of 20m/s2.
